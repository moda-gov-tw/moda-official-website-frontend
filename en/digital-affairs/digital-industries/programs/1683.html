Taiwan Smart City Development Project

Collaborating with local governments and industries, this project aims to support the implementation of smart city solutions by focusing on key areas: digital governance, digital transportation, digital agriculture and smart health care, and the global export of digital service solutions.
2025-04-16
Vertical Application Field Practice Development and Regulation Adjustment Plan

In order to promote the development of 5G vertical applications, ADI ,from 2022 to 2024, has took "the formulation of 5G dedicated frequency private network regulations and measures”, and "promoted and assisted the development of 5G dedicated frequency private network industry" as the main axes of promotion, and established the "Mobile Broadband Private Network Promote Office".
2025-04-16
Application Development and Environment Construction Program for Communication Innovation Technology

This programs is committed to serving as the development enabler for the innovative application of communication technology or data innovative application, and integrating communication industry operators, vertical application operators, information and communication equipment manufacturers, system integrators, and related ministries and civil society, etc. to complete the foundation for communication innovation applications, develop people-oriented application services and innovate business models as well as realize global cross-border applications.
2025-04-16
Next Generation Mobile Innovative Applications Empowerment Development Project

Develop 5G systems, Collaborate industry resource to provide cross-domain services.
2025-04-16
AI Application Service Development Environment Promotion Program

The two main approaches of the program are infusing “industries with AI” and “industrializing AI.” The program thus enhances smart system solutions for applications in niche areas, accelerating the development of a smart service ecosystem and helping Taiwanese businesses meet the global smart system economy.
2025-04-16
AI Talent Training Program

The program strives provide training to professional AI talents to address industries’ demands, cultivating talents for industry smart technology infusion and innovative applications with artificial intelligence as the core.

2025-04-16
Wellness of Digital Entertainment Environment and Guidance of Developer Innovation Project

Build a base environment for digital innovation, assist the domestic digital game industry, and create a friendly and high-quality business environment.
2025-04-16
The Integrated and International Intelligent Learning Industry Program

Promote the smart learning industry’s internationalization and expansion by developing education technology and environment, introducing innovative teaching and cultivation methods, consolidating industry services, and exporting to the world, thus enhancing the international competitiveness and reputation of Taiwan's smart learning industry.
2025-04-16
Smart Shalun Cyber Security Industrial Application Program

Promoting 5 major themes to demonstrate “critical infrastructure”,“smart manufacturing”, “smart green energy”,“semiconductor” and “IoT equipment,” and…
2025-04-16
Cybersecurity Industry Promotion Project

This project is based on three fundamental pillars: autonomization of cybersecurity technologies, scaling of the cybersecurity industry, and internationalization of the cybersecurity market—this program aims to strengthen Taiwan’s self-reliant cybersecurity capabilities, assist in the expansion of the domestic cybersecurity industry, and promote exports to global markets. The goal is to build a cybersecurity industry capable of supporting a digital nation and ensuring economic security.
2025-04-16
Digital Infrastructure：Cross-Domain Digital Public Infrastructure and Digital Services Ascendancy Program

The aim of this program is to construct Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI) to facilitate collaborative innovation between public and private sector organizations. It entails establishing three key foundations: AI computility, data, and code. This initiative aims to accelerate the development of digital services and reduce investment costs on software development efforts. Meanwhile, it build the working mechanism of the digital procurement, expand opportunities for digital service providers, and create an environment conducive to industrial development.
2025-04-16
Developing Smart Sensing System and Enhancing Resilience Project

This project uses Digital Twins, AIoT technology for perceptual observation and offer simulated recommendation proposals. It utilizes XR technology to enhance the resilient response measures and facilitates the establishment of resilience reinforcement in various IT industry, including reliable production, healthcare, immersive exhibition, and smart transportation. The project aims to reduce workload and hours, address the talent shortage, and mitigate the risk of industrial chain disruption.
2025-04-16
The Strategic Project of Facilitating Digital Transformation of the Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises

The project plots overall strategies, consolidates inter-agency and industry-government-academia resources, and promotes current and interdisciplinary missions according to the "2021 Technological Program Top-down Cloud Generation Industry Digital Transformation." The overall digital transformation goals of MSMEs are implemented with the aid of communication and coordination based on mutual and ongoing feedbacks and adjustments throughout the three works of "policy planning and evaluation," "resource consolidation and management," and "task and topic promotion." The project aims to achieve specific and measurable outcomes of MSMEs transformation as well as carry out the accumulation, enhancement, and spread of project data and experience.
2025-04-16
T Ambassador program

Targeting the needs of various industries, and finding talented youth that industries needed, we offer implement field and mentors lead the youth to integrate into practice and obtain international licenses. Achieving the goal of filling industrial digital talents by nurture digital seed talents.
2025-04-16
Small and Medium Enterprise Portal Accelerating Seamless Service Project(smepass)

To enhance the government service platform performance by building up a government service platform from the perspective of enterprise users which provides convenient access points for micro, small, and medium enterprises to connect the multiple cross-government services. By this means, it can ensure the efficient use of government resources and reduce enterprise administrative costs and issues. Additionally, develop innovative business models through creative data applications and management.
