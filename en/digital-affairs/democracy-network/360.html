Democracy Network

  • Share
    • Facebook
    • Twitter
    • LINE
  • Print
Connecting the international community’s and Taiwan’s digital development and democratic governance experiences, leveraging the power of democracy networks to promote civic technology and decentralized autonomy, enhancing network development digital resilience.

Operations

International Organizations

　

About Democracy Network

The department oversees democratic technology development and applications such as web3, digital net-zero by participating in international digital development organization collaborations, planning decentralized autonomy digital services, promoting data democratization, crafting net-zero application models, and preparing civic technology, open source, and reuse research environments.
