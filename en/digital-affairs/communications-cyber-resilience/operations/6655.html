The universal service of broadcast television signals in rural areas

  • Share
    • Facebook
    • Twitter
    • LINE
  • Print
The moda also assist county governments to maintain equipment of digital terrestrial television improvement stations that received subsidies, and satellite uplinks 23 broadcast television programs own by 5 TV companies (PTS, CTV, CTS, FTV and TTV) with ST-2, providing signals of TV relay for improvement stations built by county governments, PTS Taitung relay station and improvement stations own by PTS. So that citizens who live in rural areas with weak signals or without signals can watch TV programs broadcasted by 5 TV companies.
