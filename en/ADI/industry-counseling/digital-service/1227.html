Digital Platform and Service Promotion Project

  • Community Share
    • Facebook
    • Twitter
    • LINE
  • Print
Service Integration

1.Promote collaboration between IT service providers and enterprises across different industries to create user-friendly SaaS solutions tailored to their requirements, aiming to lower entry barriers and costs for small and medium-sized enterprises.

2. Assist enterprises in leveraging cloud services to enhance the value of their operational data, thereby reducing operating costs or fostering innovative service models that lead to the creation of new business opportunities.
