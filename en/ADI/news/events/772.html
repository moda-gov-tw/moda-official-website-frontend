CYBERSEC 2023, a major event for cybersecurity in the Asia-Pacific region, will take place from May 9th to 11th at the Taipei Nangang Exhibition Center, Hall 2. Administration for Digital Industries (adi), Ministry of Digital Affairs (moda) continues to support the development of domestic cybersecurity solutions. It has set up the CYBER TAIWAN PAVILION to implement the policy goal of "Six Core Strategic Industries with cybersecurity industry as a pillar."

This year’s CYBER TAIWAN PAVILION brought together 48 cybersecurity companies to display their products and services. In addition to the standard booths, the venue has four thematic exhibitions, including Zero Trust Cybersecurity, Semiconductor Cybersecurity, IoT Cybersecurity, and 5G Cybersecurity. These exhibits demonstrated corresponding solutions in working from homes, offices, situation rooms, semiconductor production lines, and bank vaults, allowing visitors to experience the importance of cybersecurity and how to apply it in practical situations.

Furthermore, CYBER TAIWAN PAVILION will host the Technology Forum during the exhibition. The topics cover zero trust, semiconductors, and offensive-defensive drills. The speakers will also share the cybersecurity consultation resources from adi, including POCs, Cybersecurity Special Interest Group Promotion, Military-Civilian technology research Program, and Cybersecurity evaluation and tests to strengthen the cybersecurity resilience of various industries in Taiwan.

The 2023 Smart City Expo will be held in Taipei and Kaohsiung, starting from March 28th and March 30th, respectively. The ADI(Administration for Digital Industries) Pavilion and Smart City Pavilion will feature Smart City Taiwan Project , together with other digital and application innovation project. This occasion aims to demonstrate smart city solutions among the topics of agriculture, health tech, governance, transportation, education , security and more, presenting Taiwan’s smart city capabilities.

Quadlink Technology, U-Sync Internet Services, Lei Si Yi Agricultural Technology, King Kit Technology, Linker Vision , Netown Corporation, and AdvMeds, Smart City Taiwan Pavilion will also join the pavilion to present Taiwan’s smart applications in a nutshell.

Date：2022/12/9-12/10 10:30-17:30

Address：Huashan 1914．Creative Park (No.1, Bade Road Sec.1, Zhong Zhen District, Taipei, Taiwan)

Event Details：

The 2022 Smart City Taiwan Carnival hosted by the Administration for Digital Industries, Ministry of Digital Affairs (ADI, moda) and the National Development Council (NDC) focused on publicizing the bountiful results of the Smart City Taiwan project and the Asia‧Silicon Valley-5G Smart Urban and Rural Industrial Application Project. Smart city solutions for themes such as Taiwan’s nationwide public healthcare, smart agriculture, education, innovative social welfare, 5G smart applications, and digital resilience were on display.

The event invited more than 30 companies and startup teams to showcase their accomplishments. The 2-day expo featured many services relevant to the general public’s daily life. Interactive amusement facilities powered by experimental smart technologies, dynamic multimedia projection shows, and parent-child workshops served to encourage public participation, inviting people to discover advancements in smart technology by participating in this technology carnival.
