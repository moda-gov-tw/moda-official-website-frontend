Operations

Cyber Security Policies and Regulations
Promoting the National Cyber Security Program and implementing the Cyber Security Management Act to build a resilient, secure, smart country.
Cyber Security Defense of Critical Infrastructure
Appointing critical infrastructure providers, promoting the implementation of legal compliance items and defense standards, and connecting competent authorities of eight major fields to expand joint national cyber security defense mechanisms.
Cyber Security Incident Reporting and Response
Implementing government agency security defense in advance, emergency response during incidents, and restoration work afterwards; coordinating regular drills to build an efficient reporting and response mechanism.
Cyber Security Exercises and Audits
Organizing cyber security audits to assist agencies in implementing Cyber Security Management Act legal compliance items, helping agencies cyber security awareness and capacity through cyber offensive and defensive exercises.
Cyber Security Education and Training
Reinforcing government cyber security personnel competence, building a comprehensive cyber security talent cultivation system, and regularly training agency cyber security personnel to produce high-quality cyber security talents.
